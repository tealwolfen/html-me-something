Video Games that taught me important features in storytelling



Transistor

Supergiant Games composer Darren Korb gives Transistor a large range music. The music changes depending on situation the characters find themselves end. The game has blue tint to it taught that music matches perfectly.



Mass Effect

While playing Mass Effect I gain a deeper apperciation for world building and giving every character motivation for their actions. Also the importance of voice actors are for adding dramactic tension do a scene.



Valkyria Chronicles

Valkyria Chronicles aesthetic puts it above many games. The story is told through journal entries of the main character. The menu is shown in the form of a book and watercolor design give the game a bedtime story effect.
